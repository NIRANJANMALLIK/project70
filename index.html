About

DAV Public School (DAV), Burla DAV Public School is alternatively also known as DAV. The school was established in 1994. DAV Public School is a Co-ed school affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). It is managed by Ananthapuri Educational Society. Burla is a locality in Sambalpur city in the state of Odisha, India. It earlier had a Notified Area Council (NAC) until 2014 after which it was included in Sambalpur city under Sambalpur Municipal Corporation (SMC). It was earlier a small town on the banks of Mahanadi. One can reach this place by road with National Highway 6 which is one of the busiest trunk routes in India as it connects Hazira to Kolkata. Hirakud Railway Station is at one end of the town and a walking distance from the Mahanadi Coal Limited (MCL).
